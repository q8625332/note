## 解密
> 比如文件是:  Ҷ-00:BE:D5:4B:3C:64.cap
> wifi名字是 Ҷ
> BSSID （路由器的 MAC 地址） 是 00:BE:D5:4B:3C:64
> cap 是 后缀


```linux
aircrack-ng -a2 -b <BSSID> -w <字典路径> <抓包文件.cap>

aircrack-ng -b 00:11:22:33:44:55 -w /usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt Ҷ-00:BE:D5:4B:3C:64.cap


```
